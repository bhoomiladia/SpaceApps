Landsat Reflectance Data at Your Fingertips

Landsat Reflectance refers to the measurement of the reflectance of Earth's surface as captured by the Landsat satellites. Reflectance is the ratio of the light energy reflected by a surface to the light energy incident on it. In the context of Landsat satellites, reflectance values are derived from the raw digital numbers (DNs) collected by the sensor, which need to be processed into physical reflectance units for analysis.
